YYtantra
Yab-Yum

The sacred union posture of Tibetan Tantra. Face to face, legs wrapped, foreheads touching. The practice is synchronized breathing and stillness.

20-40 minbeginner
chakra · breathwork · connection
  1. 1

    Active partner sits cross-legged, spine tall. Become a still mountain of presence.

  2. 2

    Receptive partner faces them, wrapping legs around the waist and arms over the shoulders.

  3. 3

    Let your chests touch. Feel the warmth between your hearts.

  4. 4

    Synchronize breath: inhale together 4 counts, exhale together 4 counts.

  5. 5

    With each inhale draw energy up the spine. With each exhale radiate from the heart.

  6. 6

    Maintain soft eye contact or rest forehead to forehead for at least 10 minutes.

💡

Play a Tibetan singing bowl recording to deepen the stillness.

Valley Orgasm

The Taoist art of expanding bliss without discharge. Rather than rushing toward a peak, you ride high arousal then consciously descend, circulating energy through the body's meridians.

45-90 minadvanced
energy cultivation · longevity · multi-orgasmic
  1. 1

    Begin with 10 minutes of connected breathing and soft eye gazing.

  2. 2

    Move into intimacy slowly, building arousal over 10-15 minutes. No rushing.

  3. 3

    As arousal approaches 70-80%, both partners PAUSE completely. Draw breath up the spine.

  4. 4

    Hold still for 1-2 minutes. Feel the energy expand into the whole body.

  5. 5

    Rest in the valley, a plateau of full-body warmth and sensitivity.

  6. 6

    Resume gently. Repeat this cycle 3-5 times before resting in integration.

Jade Garden

Balancing the yin-yang energies through gentle, wavelike, unhurried movement. Slow circular motion and conscious breath circulate chi between partners like a living river.

20-40 minbeginner
  1. 1

    Begin connected: receptive partner below, active partner above, moving very slowly.

  2. 2

    Start with circular hip movement, imagine drawing a slow figure-eight.

  3. 3

    Establish a shared breath: inhale drawing inward, exhale expanding.

  4. 4

    Active partner visualizes warm golden chi flowing from their dantian to their partner.

  5. 5

    Receptive partner receives this energy, letting it fill the pelvic bowl and radiate upward.

  6. 6

    Maintain this slow wavelike rhythm for 20-30 minutes. No pushing toward any peak.

④ Integration — 10 min

Return to complete stillness. Lie facing each other. Stay in silence for 5 full minutes, eyes open, simply being witnessed.

Kundalini Breath of Fire
5-10 min
+

A powerful Kundalini breathing technique to rapidly awaken and circulate sexual-creative energy before intimacy or as a standalone energizer.

  1. 1

    Sit facing each other, cross-legged. Make soft eye contact.

  2. 2

    Begin Breath of Fire: rapid rhythmic equal inhale-exhale through the nose. Navel pumps with each breath.

  3. 3

    Start at 1 breath per second, gradually increasing to 2-3 breaths per second.

  4. 4

    Continue for 3 minutes. Deep inhale, hold, visualize energy flooding every cell. Release slowly.

  5. 5

    Sit in silence together for 2 minutes. Notice the aliveness moving through your bodies.

Kundalini Yoga tradition, 3HO Foundation

Couples Chakra Activation
20-30 min
+

A journey through the seven energy centers together, breathwork, touch, sacred sound, and visualization from root to crown.

  1. 1

    Root: place hands on each other's knees. Tone LAM three times together.

  2. 2

    Sacral: hands to hips. Tone VAM. Feel warmth and creativity awakening.

  3. 3

    Solar Plexus: hands on each other's belly. Tone RAM. Breathe into your power.

  4. 4

    Heart: hands over each other's hearts. Tone YAM. Stay here three extra breaths.

  5. 5

    Throat and Third Eye: touch throat then forehead. Tone HAM then OM.

  6. 6

    Crown: raise joined hands above your heads. Breathe in silence for 2 full minutes.

Classical Tantric texts, adapted from Anodea Judith

Microcosmic Orbit
15-20 min
+

The foundational Taoist meditation for circulating sexual energy through the body's channels rather than discharging it. Transforms arousal into radiance.

  1. 1

    Sit comfortably, spine tall. Place your tongue to the roof of your mouth to complete the energy circuit.

  2. 2

    Breathe deeply and place awareness at your lower dantian, 3 fingers below the navel.

  3. 3

    With each inhale, visualize warm golden light rising from the dantian, up the spine, over the crown.

  4. 4

    With each exhale, guide it down the front of the body, throat, heart, belly, back to dantian.

  5. 5

    Continue for 15-20 minutes. The circuit becomes more vivid with regular practice.

Mantak Chia, Taoist Secrets of Love

Masculine Presence Training
10 min daily
+

A man's greatest gift to his partner is his undivided loving presence. This daily practice trains that capacity like a muscle.

  1. 1

    Sit in meditation. Set a timer for 10 minutes.

  2. 2

  3. 3

  4. 4

  5. 5

    Each time you drift, simply return. Presence is not a state you achieve, it is where you keep returning.
